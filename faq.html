ANSWER

QUESTION

Is there a dress code?

ANSWER

Dress in whatever makes you look and feel good! August in NYC can either be sweltering hot, raining, or—fingers crossed—perfectly temperate, so just try your best. The venue does have AC either way.

QUESTION

Is there parking at the venue?

ANSWER

No, please expect to take a car or subway.

QUESTION

I have dietary restrictions, can I make a request?

ANSWER

So do we, and we’ll be eating! Much of the dinner service will be vegan, pescatarian, gluten free, and/or soy free. If you have other concerns let us know.
