THINGS TO DO


We have a map filled with suggestions, but some general thoughts for folks who are visiting the city:

  • Bring your walking shoes!!!

  • NYC is great for wandering. Pick a direction and go!

  • Try to avoid rush hours if you're eating out, or make reservations.

  • Public transit is great and often faster than cars.

  • Not all good food is expensive! Don't sleep on corner delis and food trucks.

  • New York takes time. If you can, try to spend 4+ days in the city if you'd like to get to know it.

  • If you're staying in Brooklyn, enjoy Brooklyn! Times Square can wait.

  • If you don't want to walk all weekend you can spend a day relaxing in a park, grab a bus tour, or ride a ferry.
