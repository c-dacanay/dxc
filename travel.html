TRAVEL


The Greenpoint Loft is accessible from the Greenpoint G stop. There is no onsite parking.

LaGuardia Airport (LGA)

LaGuardia is the closest airport and an 18 minute car ride to the venue.

Hotels

We encourage you to book lodgings as soon as you're able! Try to stay in Williamsburg, Greenpoint, or Long Island City, especially if you don't want to take the subway. We've included two recommendations for nearby hotels below, but feel free to check out local AirBnBs for rooms or full apartments.

‚Ü≥Hilton Garden Inn Long Island City
This Hilton about 10-15 minutes north of the venue by car, and is located near public transit, too. This is a great option if you'd like to do some sightseeing in the city as many subway lines run through the area and it is a short ride to the Upper East Side, Central Park and the Met. It is situated near the E line which runs to the World Trade Center Memorial in Lower Manhattan and the 7 line which runs to Hudson Yards, with shopping and access to the beautiful High Line Park. Both the E and 7 stop at Times Square as well.

‚Ü≥ Henry Norman Hotel
The Norman Hotel is a great option with several different room types if you are looking to spend more time exploring Greenpoint and North Brooklyn. It is a 5-7 minute car ride from the venue, but less accessible by public transit. The G train is within walking distance and runs north to south through Brooklyn terminating just south of Prospect Park with stops in Williamsburg, Bedstuy, and Downtown Brooklyn.
